What Will I Learn?

The Biology track delivers a comprehensive study of Biology that equips students for graduate studies. Students interested in graduate work in fields such as microbiology, plant science, anatomy and physiology, or genetics should select the Biology track.

Internships and Career Preparation

Students can customize the Biology track with electives in the many areas of biology. The program’s diversity of electives enable students to focus their interest to work with plants, animals or microbes at the molecular, cellular or organismal level. Students can engage in mentored research with professors or gain experience as a lab teaching assistant. The Biology track prepares students through a Christ-centered environment. Our faculty are committed to revealing God’s creation through a Biblical perspective.

Our Graduates

Our graduates have gone on to study in fields such as microbiology and immunology. Graduates have pursued their studies at graduate schools including Virginia Tech and USC.

Choosing the Biology Track

Students should choose a Biology track by the end of their freshman year. The First Year Experience program and mentor advising will help freshman choose a track.
