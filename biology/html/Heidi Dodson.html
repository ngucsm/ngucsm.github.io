Heidi C. Dodson, Ph.D., MPH

Assistant Professor

Office: Crain 201

Office Phone:  (864) 977-2192

Email:  hdodson@ngu.edu

Courses Taught: Biochemistry, Human Biology, Integrated Biology, Metabolism, and Nutrition

Dr. Heidi Dodson came to teach at North Greenville University in 2012 as an Assistant Professor in the Biology Department. Dr. Dodson thoroughly enjoys teaching and interacting with students. She is currently one of the faculty advisors for the Biology Club, a service club led by Biology majors.

She is no stranger to the school, as she received her Bachelors degree majoring in Biology at NGU in 2005. In 2011, she completed her Ph.D. in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology from Clemson University. Her Ph.D. work involved the study of a metabolic enzyme in trypanosomes, the parasite that causes African sleeping sickness. Her interest in parasitology led her to complete a Masters in Public Health at the University of Virginia following her Ph.D.

Dr. Dodson is thankful for the opportunity she has to make a difference at NGU.
