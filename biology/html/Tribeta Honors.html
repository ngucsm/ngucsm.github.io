The Tau Delta Alpha Chapter of the Beta Beta Beta Biological Honor Society

The society emphasizes stimulation of scholarship, dissemination of scientific knowledge, and promotion of biological research. Beta Beta Beta was established as a biological honor society in 1922 and has more than 560 chapters across the country.

“We are a new chapter of Beta Beta Beta,” said Dr. Kristen van den Meiracker, faculty advisor to the chapter. “Our chapter, Tau Delta Alpha, was inducted, as well as 25 new members.”

Tribeta Membership

Students must be at least second semester sophomores, have at least nine hours of biology classes with a 3.0 or greater, and at least a 3.0 overall GPA.
