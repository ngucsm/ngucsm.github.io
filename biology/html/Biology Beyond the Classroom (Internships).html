Experiences and Internships

Learning biology is much more than studying a textbook. It's about getting involved, in the lab or in the field. Internships offer immersive and interactive experiences for learning - and to explore career opportunities. Students in each of the Biology tracks benefit from interning. Volunteering is another method to gain valuable hands-on understanding. Talk to your advisor or First Year Experience mentor about how to pursue an internship.

Student Internships

Our students have interned or volunteered with firms, physician groups, and organizations including:

  • The Greenville Zoo

  • Roper Mountain Science Center

  • Physicians with various specializations

  • New York State Energy Research and Development Authority
