Overview

The Biology program provides students with a diversity of curriculum tracks to enable students to select a focus for their Biology degree. With degree tracks in animal science, general biology, clinical emphasis, and environmental science, students will find courses that stimulate their curiosity for their career paths.

The First Year Experience program exposes entering freshmen to each curriculum track and other opportunities in the program. Students can also engage outside of the classroom through internships and experiences. The Biology Club offers networking and learning about professions in Biology, and the Tribeta Honor Society recognizes accomplished Biology students.

Choosing a Track

The Biology tracks have been developed within the Biology major to provide customized learning for students. Students should choose a Biology track by the end of their freshman year. The First Year Experience program and mentor advising will help freshman choose a track.
