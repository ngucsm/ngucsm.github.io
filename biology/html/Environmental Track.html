Internships and Career Preparation

Internships are an exciting way to explore career paths. Our Environmental Science track students have interned with organizations including The Greenville Zoo, Roper Mountain Science Center, and the New York State Energy Research and Development Authority. Working with professionals in the environmental science field presents hands-on opportunities and experiential learning.

Our Graduates

The Environmental Science track is a new track and has only recently produced its first graduates. Graduates are continuing their studies in graduate programs at Clemson University and the University of South Carolina.

Choosing the Environmental Track

Students should choose a Biology track by the end of their freshman year. The First Year Experience program and mentor advising will help freshman choose a track.
