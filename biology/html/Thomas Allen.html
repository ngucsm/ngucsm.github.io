Thomas C. Allen, MS, Ph.D., HCLD

Dean of the College of Science and Math
Professor of Biology and Chemistry
Department Head of Animal Science

Office: Dean's Office (Room 235), Crain Science Building

Office Phone:  864-977-2142

Email:  Tallen@ngu.edu

Courses Taught: Biology of Human Aging, Directed Biology Internship, Introduction to Animal Science, Freshman Biology, Human Response to Stress, Intelligent Design-Fact or Fiction, Medical Terminology, Anatomy and Physiology, Senior Research Project, Senior Seminar

Dr. Thomas C. Allen has been a full professor at North Greenville University for 19 years. He has been teaching at the collegiate level for close to 30 years. He holds an MS in Physiology / Ecology from the University of Wyoming and a Ph.D. in Human Physiology from the University of Cincinnati Medical School. He completed a post doctorate in human oncology at the University of Chicago - Ben May Institute for Cancer research. Dr. Allen taught at the University of Louisville Medical and Dental School. He has been an adjunct professor at the University of South Carolina - Upstate as well at the Medical University of South Carolina.

Dr. Allen has been awarded the South Carolina Independent College and Universities’ Excellence in Teaching Award as well as the Lamar S. Chapman Teaching Award, an honor that recognizes the most distinguished faculty at North Greenville University. He holds two patents on fertility surgical equipment and has numerous peer-reviewed publications.

Prior to his teaching years, Dr. Allen was the President and CEO of Reproductive Laboratory Associates, which was a subsidy of International Fertility Laboratory. He was Director and owner for over 13 years. He holds a certification as a High Performance Clinical Laboratory Director (HCLD). Prior to that time, he was Laboratory Director of the Infertility Laboratory at Southeastern Fertility Center in Charleston, South Carolina. He was also Co-Director of the first Andrology and Infertility Laboratory in Kentucky at the University of Louisville Medical School.

Dr. Allen is currently the Dean of the College of Science and Math and Department Head of the Animal Science program at North Greenville University. For fun, he and his wife own and manage Allen’s Ridge Ranch, where they raise Friesian horses, alpacas, and Great Danes. In the past, they have raised over 100 ostriches and 3,000 ducks for purposes of studying their breeding habits and food conversion rates. The Allens have 5 children and 15 grandchildren, 13 girls and 2 boys.
