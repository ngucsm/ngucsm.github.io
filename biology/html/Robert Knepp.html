He owned and operated his own chiropractic clinic for a few years, but felt that God had something else for his life. After selling his practice, he began teaching as a full time high school science teacher. While at the high school, he taught advanced course such as Dual Credit Anatomy and Physiology and Advance Placement Biology. Dr. Knepp grew up and has spent much of his life in Texas, except for a short stint in Arizona for high school. He is married and has four children; a seventeen year old son, two ten year old girls, and a six year son. Dr. Knepp enjoys spending time with his active family, playing golf and painting.
