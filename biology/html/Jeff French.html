Courses Taught: Ecology, Environmental Science, Natural Resource Management, and Genetics

Jeff French is chair of the Department of Biology and also assists in administration of multiple departments in the College of Science and Math. He coordinates the Environmental Science track of the Biology program and teaches courses in ecology, natural resource management, environmental science, and genetics.

Dr. French earned his Ph.D. from the University of South Carolina in 2008 after managing a graduate program there from 2000 to 2005. He came to NGU in 2007 for the opportunity to direct his academic service towards the higher service of Christ.
